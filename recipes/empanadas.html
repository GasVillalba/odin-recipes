Empanadas

This basic empanada dough can be filled with any type of filling, then fried or baked.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour for dusting
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
