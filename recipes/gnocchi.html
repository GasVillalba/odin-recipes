Gnocchi

Gnocchi are Italian dumplings made with flour, eggs, and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the potatoes, then mash or pass through a ricer.
  2. Combine the ingredients, then knead into a ball.
  3. Divide the dough into four even portions and shape into "snakes."
  4. Cut the snakes into pieces.
  5. Boil and drain the gnocchi.
